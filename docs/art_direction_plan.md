# Slap Street: Ink District Art Direction Plan

## Core Setting

The first playable location is a narrow Pattaya nightlife street at night. The tattoo shop is directly across the street from the weed shop, so the level should read as a chaotic Thai tourist-strip conflict zone rather than a generic tattoo interior.

The street is the arena. Both storefronts must be visible as major landmarks:

- Tattoo shop: red sign, tattoo flash in windows, warm interior light, scooters near the curb, plants, posters, glass reflections.
- Weed shop: green neon, cannabis/counterculture signage, brighter lime accents, stickered windows, rolling shutters, smoke/vapor glow, street customers.
- Pattaya nightlife strip: beer-bar neon, soi alley signs, massage/bar signs as background flavor, baht/currency motifs, palm silhouettes, street-food cart, tuk-tuk/songthaew hints, tangled cables, scooters, wet asphalt, curb paint, traffic cones, trash bins, puddle reflections, gritty Thai tourist-district texture.

## Visual Lock

Use the original reference photos and the Batch 1 style guide as the source of truth. Older generated sheets are reference only and should not define final production quality.

Final art should feel like:

- crisp side-scrolling arcade brawler
- readable at mobile landscape size
- neon red, cyan, purple, lime, hot pink, and warm amber accents
- strong silhouettes with consistent outline weight
- stylized characters, not photo collage
- grounded street detail without muddy darkness

Reject art that looks like a poster, collage, photobash, menu mockup, labeled concept sheet, or inconsistent AI character drift.

## Level 1 Composition

Camera target: 1280x720 mobile landscape.

World width target: 2400 to 3200 px.

The player starts near the weed shop side, crosses through the contested street, fights enemies in the road, and reaches the tattoo shop door as the level exit.

Layer plan:

- Far background: Pattaya night-strip silhouettes, palm shapes, cables, distant bar signs, alley extension.
- Mid background: weed shop storefront on one side, tattoo shop storefront across the street, both readable in the same scene.
- Play lane: wet street and curb, shallow beat-em-up depth lane, puddle reflections.
- Foreground props: scooters, cones, plants, signboards, bins, low railings, stickered chairs.
- Interactive exit: tattoo shop doorway with glow and open sign.

## Character Seed Frame Priority

Before generating any full animation sheets, create approved transparent seed frames:

1. Green Cap / Kiko
2. Big Ink / Casey
3. Indian weed shop vendor NPC
4. Indian tattoo artist NPC
5. Basic street enemy

All seed frames must use:

- 256x256 transparent PNG
- facing right
- bottom-center foot baseline
- same camera angle
- same outline weight
- same lighting direction
- same scale family
- no labels, no white background, no shadows baked onto a floor

## Animation Production Order

Only after seed frames are approved:

- Player mains: idle, walk, run, attack combo, jump/dodge, hurt, death, Super Slap.
- NPCs: idle, talk, walk, react.
- Enemies: idle, walk, attack, hurt, defeat.

Super Slap remains exclusive to Green Cap and Big Ink.

## Next Asset Batch

Batch 0 should be a consistency test, not a huge asset dump:

1. Final Green Cap seed frame.
2. Final Big Ink seed frame.
3. Level 1 street background showing the tattoo shop across from the weed shop.
4. One Indian weed shop vendor NPC seed frame.

If those four assets match, continue into full animation strips and modular environment layers.
